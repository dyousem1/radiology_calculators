⚠️ Important Disclaimer & Data Accuracy Notice
▼

Data Accuracy: This website draws on bibliometric data extracted from the Scopus database. While every effort has been made to ensure accuracy, some records may be incomplete, missing, or incorrectly attributed due to author name disambiguation challenges, duplicate author profiles, or database limitations. Overall accuracy is estimated at 90–95%. Data do not include publications indexed exclusively in PubMed, Google Scholar, Web of Science, or other databases not covered by Scopus.

Data Currency: Faculty bibliometric values (citations, publications, H-index) are refreshed approximately every 4 months from Scopus. The faculty roster is reviewed and updated annually each August to add new faculty and remove those who have retired or left academia.

AI-Assisted Development: This website and its calculators were developed with the assistance of Claude AI (Anthropic). Bibliometric data were collected programmatically via the Scopus API. The use of AI in this project is acknowledged in the associated peer-reviewed publication.

Suggest a Correction: If you find that your data — or that of a colleague — is missing, inaccurate, or misattributed, please contact the author directly. Corrections are reviewed and applied manually. 📧 [email protected]

Featuring the RY Metric

A novel bibliometric measure that weights first and last authorship 5× more than middle authorship, distinguishing research leaders from collaborative contributors.

RY = Weighted Citations + (10 × Weighted Publications)

First/Last author papers weighted 5× | Middle author papers weighted 1×
